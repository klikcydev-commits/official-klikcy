## 20-keyword structure (every major page)

1. One **primary** commercial keyword  
2. Four **service-specific** commercial terms  
3. Five **local / GEO** terms  
4. Five **AEO / search-intent** questions  
5. Five **semantic / supporting** terms  

SEO/AEO terms should be ~25% of the mix on non-SEO service pages.
